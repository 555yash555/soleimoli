Daily Itinerary
Day 1: Arrival

Arrive at Ngurah Rai International Airport. Transfer to hotel in Seminyak. Evening beach welcome party with sunset cocktails and meet fellow travelers.

Day 2: Ubud Tour

Visit Tegalalang Rice Terraces, Sacred Monkey Forest Sanctuary, and Ubud Palace. Traditional Balinese lunch included. Evening free for shopping at Ubud Art Market.

Day 3: Water Sports

Full day at Nusa Dua Beach. Try surfing, snorkeling, or parasailing. Beach BBQ lunch. Sunset watching at Uluwatu Temple with traditional Kecak dance performance.

Day 4: Island Hop

Boat trip to Nusa Penida. Visit Kelingking Beach, Angel's Billabong, and Broken Beach. Snorkeling at Crystal Bay. Return to Bali for farewell dinner.

Day 5: Departure

Free morning for relaxation or last-minute shopping. Check out from hotel and transfer to airport for departure with amazing memories.
