Itinerary

Day 1: Arrival & Beach Welcome

Arrive at Ngurah Rai Airport. Group pickup and transfer to beachfront hotel. Welcome dinner by the beach with bonfire and introductions.

Day 2: Water Sports & Snorkeling

Morning snorkeling trip to Nusa Penida. Afternoon jet skiing and paddleboarding. Evening free time at beach club.

Day 3: Temple Tour & Culture

Visit Tanah Lot and Uluwatu Temple. Traditional Balinese lunch. Watch Kecak dance performance at sunset.

Day 4: Island Hopping

Full day boat trip to Gili Islands. Snorkel with turtles, beach picnic, and sunset cruise back to Bali.

Day 5: Departure

Morning yoga session. Farewell brunch. Airport transfer and departure with lifetime memories!
